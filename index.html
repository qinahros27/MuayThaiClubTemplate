ANR Muay Thai & Fitness Center was born out of a simple idea: bringing high quality, accessible and affordable training to the entire community. Our unique approach to training and top quality faculty makes ARN Muay Thai & Fitness Center one of the best favourite training club in HCMC. We invite you to explore our engaging and diverse course schedule, and stop by for a visit at your convenience.

Muay Thai is known as the 'Art of Eight Limbs' because of its focus on 8 particular types of strikes. These eight weapons are the hands, the elbows, the knees, and the legs and feet.
